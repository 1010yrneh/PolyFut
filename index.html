Why can this take a while?

  • Analysis runs entirely on your computer — every frame is decoded and scanned locally, not in the cloud.
  • Long matches are processed in 30-second chunks; a 90-minute game can mean ~180+ chunks of detect-and-track work.
  • The engine looks for players and the ball on thousands of sampled frames so distant touches are not missed.
  • On a typical laptop CPU, a full match often needs several hours. Slow progress (e.g. chunk 40/189) is expected, not a freeze.

Tip: You can turn off the display or lock the screen — analysis keeps running on the server. You can also close this tab and tap Resume later. Only a full computer shutdown or sleep will stop the run. Keep your laptop plugged in.
